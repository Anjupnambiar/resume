Classroom Attendence Using Face Detection And Raspberry pi (2020)
In this project we use an Rfid tag and Rfid reader . When the student enters the class Rfid tag istapped on the Rfid reader placed near the door.
The information is stored in the database , camera is placed inside the classroom which captures the photo of that student whose Rfid is read and marks the attendance accordingly.
